About Me

My name’s Nia its nice to meet you, thanks for coming and checking out my work! I am currently
a third-year journalism student at the Medill School of Journalism at Northwestern University. Entering into college I was
really excited about written journalism having worked on two magazines during high school. But over time at Medill and
through exploring various extracurriculars I have found a passion for digital media storytelling, my specialties being photo
and video. There’s something that feels so real and honest about photo and video that has made me stick with it. Post-
graduation I am hoping work either in documentary journalism or fashion photography, both of which you can find
examples of on my website. If you would like to check out my full resume click here .
